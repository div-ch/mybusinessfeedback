Questions

Frequently asked.

How long does setup take?

Most pages go live within three business days. We need your logo and a contact address for your team. That is typically all we need to get started.

How do customers use it?

After a visit, your team sends the customer a short message with the link. The customer opens it, rates their experience, and shares a few words if they like. The whole thing takes less than a minute on their end.

Where does the feedback go?

Into a private Google Sheet your team has access to. Every response is logged in real time. You own the data. Nothing is stored with a third party.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on the size of your business and how many customers you serve. We share specifics on a short call. There is no long-term contract.

Is my customers' data safe?

Yes. Feedback lives in your private Google Sheet, accessible only to the people you share it with. We do not sell, resell, or use your data for anything outside of delivering your service.

Can you work with multiple locations?

Yes. We set up a dedicated page for each location, with feedback segmented so every team sees only what belongs to them. Useful for groups, franchises, and multi-site practices.

# About

> My Business Feedback is built by Nexus Multimedia, a San Diego digital marketing agency. We help businesses collect structured customer feedback and act on it.

## Built for businesses that care about the *customer experience*

Most businesses want to know how their customers feel. The problem is not a lack of interest. It is a lack of structure. There is no consistent way to ask, no reliable place for the answer to land, and no process for acting on what comes back.

My Business Feedback exists to close that gap. We give businesses a simple, branded way to hear from every customer after every visit. Not a survey. Not a review platform. A direct line between the customer and your team, designed to be short enough that people actually use it and useful enough that your team actually reads it.

The businesses we work with are not looking for marketing tools or reputation management software. They are looking for an honest signal. A way to know what is working, what is not, and where to focus their attention next.

## From the team at *Nexus Multimedia*

My Business Feedback is built and maintained by Nexus Multimedia, a digital marketing agency based in San Diego, California. Since 2016, Nexus has worked with law firms, medical practices, dental offices, home service providers, and other service businesses across the country.

That experience is what led to this product. Working closely with service businesses, we saw the same pattern again and again: teams that cared deeply about their customers but had no structured way to hear from them. Feedback was scattered across inboxes, mentioned in passing, or lost entirely.

We built My Business Feedback to solve that specific problem. Not with a complex platform or an expensive subscription. With a clean page, a simple link, and a private log that your team can actually use.

Everything about the product reflects what we learned from the businesses we have worked with. Keep it short. Keep it private. Make it easy for the customer. Make the output useful for the team. That is the entire philosophy.

## Our *approach*

### Listen well.

The best feedback systems are the ones customers actually want to use. That means keeping the interaction brief, respectful, and free of friction. One question. One tap. A comment if they want to say more. Nothing else.

### Act fast.

Feedback that sits unread for a week is feedback that has already lost its value. We deliver responses to your team in real time so you can act while the interaction is still fresh. A quick follow-up call or a same-day correction makes all the difference.

### Build relationships.

Asking a customer how their visit went is more than data collection. It is a gesture. It says your experience mattered to us, and we are paying attention. That signal, repeated consistently over time, is what turns a first visit into a long-term relationship.

Pricing scales with your business

A single-location law firm and a multi-site medical practice have different needs. We price accordingly. The $197/month starting point covers a single branded page with full functionality, real-time alerts, and ongoing support.

For businesses with multiple locations, higher volume, or more complex routing needs, pricing adjusts to match. We discuss specifics on a short call so we can recommend the right fit without guesswork.

There is no annual commitment. No setup fee. No charge for minor updates. If your business changes direction or you decide this is not the right fit, you can cancel at any time with no penalty.

Questions about pricing

Common questions

Is there a setup fee?

No. Setup is included in every plan. We build your branded page, configure your feedback log, and walk your team through the process at no additional cost.

Are there any long-term contracts?

No. All plans are month-to-month. If you need to pause or cancel, you can do so at any time. We believe you should stay because it is working, not because you are locked in.

What if I have multiple locations?

We create a dedicated page for each location, with feedback segmented so every team sees only what belongs to them. Multi-location pricing is discussed on a short call so we can tailor the setup to your organization.

What happens if I want to make changes later?

Minor updates are included in your plan. If you need to change your branding, add new team members, update routing, or adjust the questions on your feedback page, just let us know and we will take care of it.

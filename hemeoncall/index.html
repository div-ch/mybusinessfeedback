Questions

Frequently asked.

How long does setup take?

Most client pages go live within three business days of kickoff. We need your logo, your Google review link, and a support contact where unhappy feedback should be routed. That's it.

Is this suppressing negative reviews?

No. Customers can still leave a Google review at any time, regardless of what they choose on our page. What we do is give unhappy customers a faster, more useful path to your team, and happy customers an easier path to Google. Most businesses find that when issues are resolved privately and quickly, customers are less motivated to post publicly.

Where does the feedback data live?

In a Google Sheet you own. We log every rating, every path taken, and every message submitted, in real time. Your team gets view access. Nothing is stored with a third party.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on your business size and volume. We'll share specifics during a short discovery call. There is no long-term contract. Month-to-month.

Do I have to send the links manually?

For now, yes. We provide template copy for text and email so your team can send in thirty seconds. Automated sending through CRMs and scheduling platforms is on the roadmap.

What if a customer still leaves a bad Google review?

That's where our parent agency, Nexus Multimedia, comes in. We handle review removal, reputation defense, and response strategy for our clients as a separate service.

Can I white-label this for my agency?

Agency partnerships are available for select partners who manage multiple businesses. Reach out and tell us about your book.
